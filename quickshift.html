Business Pitch: Quick Shift

Overview

For an entrepreneurial class I took, I was given the task to come up with a business and pitch the business. What we came up with was an app/website called Quickshift. QuickShift is a dynamic platform connecting local businesses with immediate staffing needs to college students seeking flexible, on-demand work opportunities in East Lansing. We thought that this would be a really valuable resource for students. With this project we dove into all of the steps it takes to create your own business and went through the experience ourselves.
